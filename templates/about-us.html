Who we are

At SUCCYLOGLOBALFX, our mission is to create the world’s best exchange platform for individuals and international businesses. SUCCYLOGLOBALFX was born back in 2016 by innovators in a Moscow basement armed with ten years of banking experience, an entrepreneurial spirit, and a desire to provide customers a real alternative to the big banks. Since then, we’ve grown exponentially, and with a global team around 600 strong, have become a market-leading, multi-award winning, bank-beating, rapidly-growing, fun-loving international payments company. Currently, more than 10,000 global customers & 15,000 global businesses choose SUCCYLOGLOBALFX for their international transfers. We have transferred £70 Million for our customers since we launched, more than 1 million transfers per year
